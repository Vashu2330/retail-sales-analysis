## Project Overview

End-to-end exploratory data analysis of 500 retail orders across 4 regions, 5 product categories, and 12 Indian cities for the year 2023.

## Tools & Technologies
- **Python** — Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn
- **Excel** — Pivot tables, charts, formatted dashboard (openpyxl)
- **Jupyter Notebook** — Full analysis with inline visualizations

## Key Findings
- Total Revenue: ₹6.2M+ across 500 orders
- Electronics is the top revenue category; Office Supplies has the best profit margin
- West region (Mumbai, Pune) leads in sales contribution
- Discounts above 15% significantly reduce profit margins — recommendation: cap at 10%
- Corporate segment generates 35%+ higher average order values than Consumer
